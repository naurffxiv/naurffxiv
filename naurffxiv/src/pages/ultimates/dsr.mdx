# Dragonsong's Reprise (Ultimate)
## Strats by Phase
### P1: Adelphel, Grinnaux and Charibert
- [Vault Knights Toolbox](https://ff14.toolboxgaming.space/?id=802461081524561&preview=1#1)
- Interrupt order: Adel tank > phys range > Adel tank  
- Protean Baits: H > M > R > T
- Puddle Drops: Drop E/W Boss Relative. Alternate each drop.
### P2: King Thordan
- [Start + Strength of the Ward Raidplan](https://raidplan.io/plan/NDX-6bF2djrDMB-v) 
- [Sanctity of the Ward pt. 1 - Drk Relative Dodges Raidplan](https://raidplan.io/plan/2CYKJu6IHzBdolZu)
- [Sanctity of the Ward pt. 2 - Meteors Raidplan](https://raidplan.io/plan/Src8CpFxCAUUuZEM)
- Meteor role takes outer towers.
### P3: Nidhogg
- [Nidhogg Toolbox](https://ff14.toolboxgaming.space/?id=267799249146271&preview=1)
- Westhogg (Arrows face West)
- Enumeration Adjust Priority: CW > CCW > Diagonal
### P4: The Eyes
- [The Eyes Toolbox](https://ff14.toolboxgaming.space/?id=613699253336171&preview=1)
- G1 NW/CCW, G2 NE/CW 
- 3rd Dive Swaps follow 1st Dive priority.
### Intermission: Rewind
- [Intermission Toolbox](https://ff14.toolboxgaming.space/?id=999851866521561&preview=1)
- Tank LB as soon as Haurchefant appears
### P5: King Thordan II
**Wrath of the Heavens**
- [Wrath Toolbox](https://ff14.toolboxgaming.space/?id=514795714993271&preview=1)
- Knights on outside = Relative North.
- Blue Defam: 4 notches NW or line below marker.
- Divebomb = opposite of the WAR (behind mage).
- Lightnings towards wall, party in front of WAR.

**Death of the Heavens**

- [DOTH Toolbox](https://ff14.toolboxgaming.space/?id=414790163993271&preview=1)
- WAR is relative North.
- Inner Dooms NW/NE, Outer Dooms E/W.
- Outer Dooms bait 'Circle,' then move.
