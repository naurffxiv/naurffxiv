## Party Finder Terms

### P1/3 Raidplan

Phases 1 and 3 follow raidplans created by the Toxic Friends group.

- [Toxic Friends - M7S P1](https://raidplan.io/plan/PWFgPfz0vL7runO2)
- [Toxic Friends - M7S P3](https://raidplan.io/plan/DEijc3hhq_CNGaRg)

Hector also showcases these strategies in a video guide, which can be found in the video guide section below.

### Locked Seeds

Locked Seeds is a deviation from the Toxic Friends suite of raidplans.
This strategy is used for phase 2 and is the preferred strategy.

- [Locked Seeds Raidplan](https://raidplan.io/plan/FGvPnlaAe1fwlsIB)

Just like the Toxic Friends raidplans, Hector also showcases this strategy in a video guide,
which can be found in the video guide section below.
