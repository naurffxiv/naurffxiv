# M8S - Howling Blade (Savage)

![Howling Blade](/images/thumbnails/savage/aac_cw/m8s.png)

## Party Finder Summary

```
P1: Millenial Fering - Tera Clock - Quad Moonlight | P2: TF + Rinon Wolf's Lamnent/UV4
```

## Party Finder Terms

M8S strategies are mix and matched from multiple sources.
All raidplans used for M8S can be found in the [M8S Gigabin](https://pastebin.com/xT2gAw2R).

### Phase 1

#### Fering Millenial

Prior to the Millennial Decay mechanic, NA PF follows the [Toxic Friends - M8S P1 Raidplan](https://raidplan.io/plan/46uVU6o49FPuYXOs).

'Fering Millenial' refers to the [FERINGBIN M8S Millennial Decay Raidplan](https://raidplan.io/plan/IRl6bjA5WzSweStq)
where Healers + Melee are West, Tanks and Range East; G1 N, G2 S.

#### Tera Clock

Refers to the [Clockspot Terrestrial Rage Raidplan](https://raidplan.io/plan/s3EIvZnk3qvuFy86).

This strat handles the mechanic at/around your clock spot in a TN fashion.
Stack 1 will be under the boss, Stack 2, will be either N or NE.

#### Quad Moonlight

Refers to the [Quad Moonlight Raidplan](https://raidplan.io/plan/WFsLBku1C9Iyxneu).

This strat is handled in the safe quadrants from the 1st & 2nd cleave, then the 3rd 4th cleave.
Stack is at the wall, spreads are melees front, range back with LP 1 Left & LP2 Right.

### Phase 2

#### TF + Rinon Wolf's Lamnent/UV4

NA PF follows the [M8S - Toxic/Rinnon Raidplan](https://raidplan.io/plan/Zzf38hNz9aFrn-6T) for the entirety of phase 2.
