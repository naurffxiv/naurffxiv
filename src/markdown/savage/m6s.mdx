## Party Finder Terms

### Hector/Raidplan

The majority of the fight follows the strategies showcased in the [Toxic Friends Raidplan](<https://raidplan.io/plan/Pgj53K49w8LAZpI6>),
which is often referred to as 'Hector' since his video follows the same strategies.

Hector's video guide can be found below in the video guides section.

### Cleavemaxxing

Cleavemaxxing is a strategy separate from the Toxic Friends Raidplan.
This strategy maximizes player cleave damage during the adds phase. 

Original Raidplan: [Cleavemaxxing](<https://raidplan.io/plan/ywV9cu6GRQ68SQLy>)

A mini-guide by Hector can be found in the video guides section below.
